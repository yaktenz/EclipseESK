1. How to start Store Robberies

Robberies

It’s very easy. The payout is nearly 20k every 10-15 mins. All you need is a gun and a robber outfit with gloves and a mask with an extra set to switch into after you rob it. You just need 2 people one to hold the cashier at gunpoint and have the other steal the cash at the register. Once you quit aiming cops are alerted. It’s best to enable toggle to aim through settings. After getting packed money follow through the steps in #loca

1. Getting Started: How To Play EclipseRP

Welcome to the world! This guide walks you through the essential steps to get your account set up and prepare for your first roleplay session on EclipseRP.

Installing RageMP

To play Eclipse RP on Rage, download and install the RAGE Multiplayer client, then use the in-game server list or direct connect to find and join "ECLIPSE ROLEPLAY ENGLISH SERVER". Before you can fully play, you must complete the roleplay application, which involves answering questions about server rules and scenarios.

Step 2: Connect To EclipseRP

Launch the RAGE Multiplayer client. In the server list, search for "ECLIPSE ROLEPLAY" or "Eclipse Roleplay English Server". Alternatively, You can use the direct connect feature and enter the address: play.eclipse-rp.net:22005

Step 3: Complete the roleplay application

Once you connect, you will be prompted to complete a roleplay application. Answer the questions related to server rules and roleplay scenarios. Submit your answers for review by the staff team, which can take up to 24 hours. But they accept almost everybodys within the time you get done making your character

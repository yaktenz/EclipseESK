Server Rules

Welcome to the server! These rules are designed to ensure a fun, safe, and respectful environment for all members. By staying in this server, you agree to abide by these guidelines.

1. RESPECT AND GENERAL CONDUCT (Zero Tolerance)
02
1.1. No Harassment or Discrimination Treat all members with respect. Any form of personal attack, bullying, or harassment is strictly forbidden.
03
1.1. (Cont.) Absolute Zero Tolerance Zero Tolerance for hate speech, racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, or discrimination based on age, religion, or disability.
04
1.1. Specific Slur Penalty Use of hard-R racial slurs results in an immediate 1-Hour Timeout Minimum, followed by escalated review for a permanent ban.
05
1.2. Maintain a Positive Environment Keep discussions constructive. Excessive negativity, gatekeeping, or instigating arguments (flaming) is not permitted.
06
1.2. (Cont.) Offensive Media Do not post messages, memes, or media that are intentionally offensive, crude, or designed to provoke a negative reaction.

08
2. CONTENT AND SHARING Adherence to content policies is strictly enforced.
09
2.1. Strict Anti-Spam/Flooding Policy No Spamming: Do not flood text channels with rapid-fire messages, overly long paragraphs, or excessive emojis/reactions.
10
2.1. (Cont.) Excessive Tagging Do not intentionally tag staff, moderators, or general members repeatedly or unnecessarily. Use channels for their intended purpose.
11
2.2. No External Links (Strict Policy) For the safety and security of all members, NO EXTERNAL LINKS are permitted, period. This includes all clickable URLs and invite links.
12
2.2. (Cont.) Resource Sharing If you need to share a resource, please ask a Moderator for assistance, and they may share it on your behalf after verification.
13
2.3. No NSFW, Gore, or Illegal Content Pornographic, excessively graphic (gore), or disturbing content is strictly forbidden in all channels.
14
2.3. (Cont.) Illegal Activities Content promoting or discussing illegal activities (piracy, hacking, self-harm) results in an immediate ban and reporting.
15
2.4. No Self-Promotion or Advertising Do not promote your own Discord server, Twitch stream, YouTube channel, business, products, or services anywhere on the server.

3. SERVER INTERACTION & SUPPORT Guidelines for engaging with the server features and staff.
18
3.1. Choosing and Displaying Roles Visit the designated channel (e.g., 「-✦-claim-roles-✦-」) to select your appropriate roles for access.
19
3.2. Respect Staff and Decisions The Moderation Team and Staff decisions are final. Address issues privately and respectfully with a higher-ranking staff member.
20
3.2. (Cont.) No Backseat Moderating If you see a rule broken, use the reporting tools or create a ticket—do not attempt to enforce the rules yourself.
21
3.3. How to Get Support (Ticketing) If you require assistance from staff, create a ticket in the designated channel (e.g., 「-✦-support-ticket-✦-」).
22
3.4. Ticket Detail Requirement (MANDATORY) When opening a ticket, you MUST immediately provide detailed context in the first message. Bad Example: "Help."
